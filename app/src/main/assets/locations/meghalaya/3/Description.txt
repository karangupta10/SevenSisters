Dawki needs no introduction. It is one of the most popular tourist places in Meghalaya. 
Known for its clear river, Dawki is one of the most instagrammable places on earth. 
If you are visiting Meghalaya anytime soon, then you should put Dawki on your itinerary. 
Since it is sandwiched between India and Bangladesh, it is also a major trade hub.

Speciality: Gorgeous river with clear waters.

Location: West Jaintia Hills district, Meghalaya

Best time to visit: November
