A tropical rainforest located amid the Nongrum region of Meghalaya, Mawphlang Sacred Forest houses rich flora and fauna. 
Towering trees, which are said to be 1000 years old, encompasses this forest. 
The eye-pleasing greenery of this forest reflects the natural grandeur of Meghalaya. 
The forest also holds mythological significance to the local community hence it is considered sacred by them. 
Undoubtedly, the forest offers an astounding escape to nature lovers and is one of the prominent attractions amongst all the Meghalaya tourist places.

Speciality: Lush greenery

Best time to visit: April to June
