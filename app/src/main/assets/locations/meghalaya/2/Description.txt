Cherrapunji is regarded as one of the wettest places on earth as the highest rainfall is foreseen during the year.
Tourists from around the world come here to see the Dain-Thlen, Kynrem, and Nohkalikai waterfalls that are known as the popular waterfalls of Cherrapunji. 
The town encompasses some of the best waterfalls in Meghalaya.

Major attractions here are Living-Root bridges, Mawsmai cave, Seven sisters falls, Eco-park and a garden of caves.
