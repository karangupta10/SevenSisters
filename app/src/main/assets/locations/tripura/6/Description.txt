One of the famous tourist places in Tripura, Neermahal Palace is sitting right in the heart of Rudrasagar Lake. 
This Palace not only gives one a chance to witness a scintillating structure but also beholds the beauty of its surroundings. 
The name translates to ‘water palace’ in English and justifies the name to the T. 
When it comes to the seven wonders of this country, Neermahal Palace found itself a spot in the list. 
Founded by Martin & Burns Co., this Palace is home to a royal Antar Mahal, a guest room, a playing room, a courtyard, and much more!
