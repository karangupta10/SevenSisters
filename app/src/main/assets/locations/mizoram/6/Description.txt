Are you ready to experience the true Mizoram? You must head to Falkawan Village. 
This is one of the best places to visit in Mizoram and it gives you a clear picture of the Mizo tribe and their lifestyle. 
This is a village that would give you an insight into the entire state of Mizoram and going on a cultural tour to this village is a must. 
Especially if you are interested in culture, history and some fascinating tales about a place then, Falkawan village is an apt destination in Mizoram.

Places to visit: Salvation Army Temple, Mizoram state museum

Best season to visit: November to March
