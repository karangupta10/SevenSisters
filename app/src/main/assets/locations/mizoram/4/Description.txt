Experience the lovely village life basking in the sun amidst the lush green fields of Reiek. 
For an offbeat experience in Mizoram, one must visit this village to enjoy to the fullest. 
If you are a traveler and want to feel every single second of your life then, this is the place you must live at for a few weeks. 
A walk in this village would leave you rejuvenated totally.

Places to visit: Reiek Village exploration

Best time to visit: November to March
