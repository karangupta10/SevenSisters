Have you ever dreamt of a paradise on earth? Then, that dream is going to turn into reality at Aizawl. 
This is one of the oldest cities of Mizoram and attracts a lot of tourists because of the picturesque views it offers. 
The tranquil setup of this quaint town can leave your mind, body, and soul totally relaxed. 
This town has plenty of Mizoram tourist places for visitors. One can also choose to hike up to the nearby hilly areas for a panoramic sight of the whole town. 
If get a chance, attend the vibrant Mizoram festivals that take place in Aizawl.

Places to visit: Hmuifang, Tamdil Lake, Chanmari, Handicraft museum

Best time to visit: November to March
