Mizoram boasts of having the 13th highest waterfall in the country, Vantawng waterfalls. 
This is one of the major tourist attractions in Mizoram. 
Watching the waterfalls gushing from a height between the valleys and the dense jungles is a sight to behold. 
The spectacular view of the Mizoram waterfalls can leave you in trance and give you an unforgettable experience.

Places to visit: Chhingpui Thlang

Best season to visit: September to January
