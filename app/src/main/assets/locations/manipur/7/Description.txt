Manipur has a bounty of picturesque waterfalls one of which is Leimaram Waterfall. 
It has become a tourist hotspot because of its enchanting views and tranquil surroundings. 
There is not just one but three waterfalls to greet you in their glory. 
Enjoy the refreshing sprinkle of water as you go near the falls. 
It is one of the best places to visit in Manipur, India that you ought to visit.

Attractions near Leimaram Waterfalls: Loukoi Paat Lake, Langthabal, Iskcon Temple

Best time to visit: October to March
