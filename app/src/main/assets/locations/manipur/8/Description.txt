Located around 48 km away from Imphal, Loktak boasts of being the largest freshwater lake in North East India. 
The lake can be seen from the tourist bungalow set atop Sendra Island.
 The shimmering blue waters and the colourful water plants make it look beautiful.
 Designated as a wetland of international importance under the Ramsar Convention, the lake has an important role in the economy of Manipur.

Best time to visit : February- March
