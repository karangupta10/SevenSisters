A place that pays a tribute to the military, Khongjom is one of the best Manipur destinations. 
Right here, the revered major general, Paona Brajabashi with his troops fought with British during their reign.
 The was is known as Anglo-Manipur War. 
Today there is a memorial built in remembrance of the brave fighters, which holds historical importance. 
The place is perfect for an Indian patriot, or anyone with interest in military and the history of wars.

Attractions in Khongjom: Khongjom War Memorial

Best time to visit: October to March
