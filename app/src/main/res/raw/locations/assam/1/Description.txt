A dreamy town set in the oscillating hills and lush valleys of Dima Hasao, Haflong is often referred to as the “Scotland of Assam”.
Enriched with stunning hills, lofty terrains, and a sparkling lake, Haflong is among the best places to see in Assam for camping enthusiasts, hikers and trekkers, and peace seekers.

Highlights: Hills, Adventure

Prime Attractions: Jatinga, Haflong Lake, Maibong, Silchar, Jatinga
